Where It All Started

We are a second-generation family owned auto repair shop with 40 years of experience. Started in 1976 by Stephen Smutny Sr. and BOB? Ross as a small transmission and body shop, we have since grown into one of the most well known auto repair shops in Palmetto Bay. This tradition of excellence and honesty is carried on by the second-generation Smutnys, Steve and Bill.

We work hard to give our customers the best experience possible. At our shop, the customer always comes first, and we are always honest and open about what your car really needs, and what it doesn't. For over 40 years we have built a reputation of excellence, and we will keep that level of service with every single customer that walks through our doors.
